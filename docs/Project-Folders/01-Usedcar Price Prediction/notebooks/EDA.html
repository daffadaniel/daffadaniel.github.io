This dataset representing a unique vehicle listing, and includes twelve distinct features providing valuable insights. - Brand: Identify the brand or company name - Model: Specific model of each vehicle - model_year: Manufacturing year of the vehicles, crucial for assessing depreciation and technology advancements - mileage: Mileage of each vehicle, a key indicator of wear and tear and potential maintenance requirements - fuel_type: Type of fuel the vehicle runs on (gasoline, diesel, electric, hybrid, etc.) - engine: Engine specifications - transmission: Transmission type (automatic, manual, or other variants) - ext_col: Exterior color of the car - int_col: Interior color of the car - accident: Vehicle prior history of accidents or damage - clean_title: Indicates whether the vehicle has a clean title (legal status) - price: Price of the vehicle (target variable)
